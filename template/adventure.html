Kerala: Munnar - Serene Hill Station

Munnar, nestled in the lush Western Ghats, is a picturesque hill station known for its tea plantations, misty mountains, and tranquil lakes. Explore the breathtaking landscapes, go trekking in the Anamalai hills, and indulge in Ayurvedic treatments amidst nature's splendor.

Himachal Pradesh: Shimla - Queen of Hill Stations

Shimla, the erstwhile summer capital of British India, is renowned for its colonial architecture, scenic beauty, and toy train ride on the UNESCO-listed Kalka-Shimla Railway. Enjoy panoramic views of the Himalayas, stroll along the Mall Road, and indulge in adventure activities like trekking and skiing.

Santorini, Greece

Meghalaya: Mawlynnong

Dubbed as "Asia's Cleanest Village," Mawlynnong is a picturesque village tucked away in the East Khasi Hills of Meghalaya. Surrounded by dense forests and cascading waterfalls, this eco-friendly village offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape. Visitors can stroll along treehouse pathways, visit the living root bridges, and experience the warm hospitality of the local Khasi people.

Rome, Italy

With its ancient ruins, historic landmarks like the Colosseum and Vatican City, charming streets, and delicious cuisine, Rome is a treasure trove of art, history, and culture.
